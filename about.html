About Me

I'm a French engineer trained in mathematics and computer science. I have always enjoyed math, physics and computer science, and I am especially passionate about computer graphics.

Back in high school, I was already plotting unusual functions and writing little programs for my calculator. So I was amazed when I stumbled upon Shadertoy and discovered the possibilities that shaders (and math) could offer.

Ever since, I have been exploring this vast domain and many of the techniques it employs. I have had a lot of fun with personal projects, and I am also interested in physics simulation and the fascinating results it can yield. I am eager to find new challenging problems to solve while having a meaningful purpose to my work.

Aside from that, I started climbing when I was ten years old (in the calanques of Marseille). I have kept at it ever since and have even taken part in a few climbing contests. I'm also an avid hiker.
